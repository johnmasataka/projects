LA Brooktree House

Location: Los Angeles, California, USA

Program: Residential

Year: 2023

Team: John Jiang

Info: Modern Single-Family Residence

The LA Brooktree House is a contemporary residential project that reimagines modern living in the heart of Los Angeles. The design emphasizes clean lines, natural light, and seamless indoor-outdoor connections, creating a sophisticated yet comfortable living environment.

The architecture responds to the site's unique topography and climate, incorporating sustainable features and passive design strategies. Large windows and sliding glass doors frame views of the surrounding landscape while promoting natural ventilation and reducing energy consumption.

Interior spaces are thoughtfully arranged to maximize functionality and flow, with an open-plan layout that encourages social interaction while maintaining private areas for retreat. High-quality materials and careful detailing throughout reflect a commitment to craftsmanship and durability.
