Mediterranean Restaurant

Location: Honolulu, Hawaii, USA

Program: Commercial & Hospitality

Year: 2023

Team: John Jiang

Info: Fine Dining Restaurant

The Mediterranean Restaurant in Honolulu brings the warmth and elegance of Mediterranean dining culture to the tropical paradise of Hawaii. The design creates a seamless blend between indoor and outdoor spaces, taking advantage of the island's perfect climate while providing comfortable, sheltered dining areas.

The restaurant's architecture draws inspiration from both Mediterranean vernacular and Hawaiian traditions. Natural materials like stone, wood, and textured plaster create a rich, tactile environment, while large openings and covered terraces maintain a connection to the surrounding landscape and ocean views.

The interior design focuses on creating intimate dining spaces through thoughtful spatial arrangement and lighting design. Custom furniture and carefully selected finishes reflect the restaurant's commitment to authenticity and quality, while flexible spaces accommodate both casual and formal dining experiences.
