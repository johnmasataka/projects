Kigumi

Location: Okinawa, Japan

Program: Furniture Design

Year: 2023

Team: John Jiang

The Kigumi furniture collection draws inspiration from traditional Japanese wood joinery techniques, particularly those found in Okinawan architecture. Each piece in the collection celebrates the sophisticated craftsmanship of wood joinery, where pieces interlock perfectly without the use of nails or screws.

The collection includes chairs, tables, storage units, and decorative screens, all constructed using locally sourced Okinawan woods. The designs emphasize the beauty of traditional joinery methods while incorporating contemporary functionality and aesthetics, creating pieces that are both practical and artistically significant.

Developed in collaboration with local master craftsmen, the collection preserves and promotes traditional woodworking techniques while exploring new applications in modern furniture design. Each piece showcases the precision and beauty of kigumi joinery, creating furniture that is not only functional but also serves as a testament to this enduring craft tradition.
