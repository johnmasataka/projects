Aman Miami Beach

Location: Miami Beach, Florida, USA

Program: Luxury Hotel & Residences

Year: 2024

Team: Kengo Kuma and Associates

Info: New Construction, 15,000 sqm

Aman Miami Beach is a luxury hotel and residential development that seamlessly integrates with the vibrant culture and tropical landscape of Miami Beach. The design draws inspiration from the natural elements of the coastal environment while incorporating Aman's signature minimalist aesthetic.

The project features a series of interconnected pavilions, private gardens, and water features that create a sense of tranquility within the urban setting. The use of natural materials, including local stone and sustainable wood, combined with floor-to-ceiling windows maximizes the connection to the surrounding landscape and ocean views.
