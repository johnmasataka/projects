Ko Olina

Location: Honolulu, Hawaii, USA

Program: Hospitality & Residential

Year: 2023

Team: John Jiang

Info: Luxury Resort & Residential Development

The Ko Olina project represents a harmonious blend of luxury hospitality and residential spaces in the pristine coastal setting of Oahu, Hawaii. This development is designed to create an immersive experience that celebrates the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the Hawaiian islands.

Drawing inspiration from traditional Hawaiian architecture and the surrounding landscape, the design incorporates organic forms and local materials to create a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor spaces. The project emphasizes sustainability and environmental stewardship while providing world-class amenities and comfort.

The residential component features thoughtfully designed units that maximize ocean views and natural ventilation, while the hospitality spaces offer a range of experiences from intimate garden retreats to grand oceanfront gathering areas. The architecture responds to the local climate through deep overhangs, strategic orientation, and careful consideration of sun patterns and prevailing winds.
