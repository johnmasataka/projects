What is Manav?

Manav is an innovative Grasshopper plug-in specifically designed to generate ergonomic furniture based on human postures. This tool accommodates a wide spectrum of poses, ranging from everyday positions to extreme and unconventional ones, such as handstands or mid-cartwheel. By analyzing these diverse postures, Manav creates customized furniture tailored to the specific body configurations of users, thereby offering a harmonious blend of functionality and creativity in furniture design.

Key Benefits

  • Rapid Prototyping: Designers and manufacturers can swiftly prototype unique furniture solutions tailored to specific users or applications, significantly reducing traditional design process time.
  • Customized and Adaptive Furniture: Generate fully customized furniture that accommodates a wide range of postures, ensuring unparalleled comfort and support.
  • Inclusivity and Accessibility: Facilitates the design of more inclusive furniture options that cater to individuals with diverse physical needs or disabilities.

Who Is It For?

  • Furniture Designers and Manufacturers: Create customizable, ergonomic, or avant-garde furniture that adapts to unique user needs.
  • Workplace and Office Solutions: Optimize workspaces for comfort and productivity, supporting diverse working styles and body types.
  • Healthcare and Rehabilitation: Design customized supportive furniture for various postures and mobility challenges in healthcare settings.

Technical Components

Manav consists of three main parts:

  • Posture Rigging: Features both freeform dragging and parameterized approaches for intuitive mannequin manipulation.
  • Furniture Generation: Includes assembly furniture, adaptive furniture, and soft-body furniture generation methods.
  • Furniture Analysis: Provides comprehensive analysis of ergonomics and structural integrity using advanced visualization techniques.
