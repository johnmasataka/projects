Aman Bora Bora

Location: Bora Bora, French Polynesia

Program: Hospitality & Resort

Year: 2023

Team: John Jiang

Info: Luxury Resort & Overwater Villas

Aman Bora Bora represents the pinnacle of luxury hospitality in French Polynesia, harmoniously blending contemporary design with traditional Polynesian architecture. The resort comprises overwater villas, beachfront suites, and hillside pavilions, each offering unique perspectives of the lagoon's crystal-clear waters and Mount Otemanu.

The architectural design emphasizes sustainability and cultural sensitivity, utilizing local materials and traditional construction techniques while incorporating modern comfort and technology. The overwater villas feature innovative structural systems that minimize environmental impact on the delicate marine ecosystem, while their design maximizes natural ventilation and views.

Interior spaces reflect a sophisticated interpretation of Polynesian design elements, with high-pitched roofs, natural textures, and seamless indoor-outdoor transitions. The project demonstrates how luxury hospitality can enhance rather than compromise the natural environment, setting new standards for sustainable resort development in sensitive marine locations.
